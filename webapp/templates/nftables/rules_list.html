About nftables Rules

This page shows the current nftables firewall configuration. nftables is the modern Linux firewall framework.

  • Tables: Organize related rules (filter, nat, etc.)
  • Chains: Process packets at specific points (input, output, forward)
  • Rules: Define what to do with matching packets (accept, drop, etc.)
